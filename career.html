Career Support

Explore Career Pathways and Support

UMSI offers a comprehensive range of career resources to help you navigate your job search, secure internships, and connect with potential employers. Whether you’re seeking guidance on career planning, interview preparation, or professional development, our dedicated career coaches and employer connections are here to support your success.

Take advantage of workshops, one-on-one coaching sessions, and networking events designed to prepare you for the competitive job market and help you achieve your career goals.

Career FAQ

  • How do I find internships?
    Utilize UMSI’s internship listings, attend career fairs, and connect with career coaches for personalized advice.
  • How do I connect with employers?
    Participate in networking events, employer info sessions, and leverage UMSI’s employer connections platform.
  • What support does UMSI offer for job searches?
    UMSI provides job search workshops, resume and interview coaching, and access to job postings tailored to information professionals.
  • When should I start looking for full-time roles?
    Begin your search early—typically 3-6 months before graduation—to maximize opportunities and preparation time.
